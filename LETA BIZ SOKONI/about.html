About Leta Biz Sokoni

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Leta Biz Sokoni started as a small online store with a vision to make shopping convenient for Kenyans. Today, we've grown to become one of the leading e-commerce platforms in the country, offering a wide range of products from electronics to home appliances.

Our Mission

To provide quality products at affordable prices with exceptional customer service. We aim to make online shopping accessible, reliable, and enjoyable for everyone in Kenya.

Why Choose Us?

  • Wide selection of quality products
  • Competitive prices
  • Fast and reliable delivery
  • Secure payment options
  • Excellent customer service
